## Can You Register a Rent Agreement Completely Online in Maharashtra?

Yes. Maharashtra was one of the first states to offer online Leave & License registration through the Department of Registration & Stamps. The process involves:

- Online agreement drafting and stamp duty payment
- Aadhaar-based biometric e-KYC verification (done at your location by an authorized agent)
- Digital submission to the government registration portal
- Receiving a digitally signed registered agreement

The entire process requires **no physical visit to any Sub-Registrar office.** Both parties (landlord and tenant) complete biometric verification from the comfort of their respective locations.

## Step 1: Gather Required Documents

Before starting, ensure you have the following ready:

**Landlord:** Aadhaar card, PAN card, property ownership proof (sale deed, share certificate, or property tax receipt), passport photo

**Tenant:** Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport photo

**Property details:** Full address, carpet area, flat/unit number, CTS/property number

If you use Prime Document Solutions, our team will collect all details through a guided WhatsApp or online form and verify completeness before proceeding.

## Step 2: Agreement Drafting

A legally compliant Leave & License agreement must include all mandatory clauses as per the Maharashtra Rent Control Act. Our legal team prepares a customized draft that covers:

- Monthly licence fee and escalation clauses
- Security deposit amount and refund conditions
- Lock-in period and early termination notice
- Maintenance responsibilities
- Permitted use of property
- Prohibited activities
- Dispute resolution clause

The draft is shared with both parties for review and approval before proceeding to stamp duty payment.

## Step 3: Stamp Duty Calculation and Payment

Once the draft is approved, stamp duty is calculated based on:

- Monthly rent × number of months = total licence fee
- Plus security deposit
- **0.25% of total consideration = stamp duty amount** (minimum ₹500)

Stamp duty is paid online through the Maharashtra government's eStamp system. The eStamp certificate is generated immediately and attached to the agreement.

A registration fee (1% of the licence amount, maximum ₹30,000) is also paid at this stage.

## Step 4: Aadhaar Biometric e-KYC

This is the most critical step. An authorized biometric verification agent from Prime Document Solutions visits your location (home or office) with a registered POS biometric device.

Both the landlord and tenant must be present at their respective locations (they can be at different places on the same day or on different days, as our agent schedules both visits).

The biometric device captures fingerprints and/or iris scans, which are verified against the Aadhaar database in real time. The verified eKYC data is electronically submitted to the government registration portal.

**Note:** Both parties must be present in person for biometric verification. This step cannot be done remotely.

## Step 5: Digital Registration & Certificate Delivery

Once biometric verification is successfully completed, our team submits the fully executed, stamped agreement to the Maharashtra government registration portal.

The Sub-Registrar digitally processes and endorses the registration. You receive:

1. A digitally signed registered Leave & License agreement (PDF)
2. Registration number and details
3. Delivery via WhatsApp and email **within 24–48 hours** of biometric completion

This document is the official registered copy and is acceptable as proof of address, legal evidence, and for all official purposes.

The total cost includes three components:
